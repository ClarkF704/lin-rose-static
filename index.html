    AWARD-WINNING PREMIER HOME STAGING

    Domaine Staging is an award-winning premier home staging company in the Carolinas. Domaine started back in 2006, before many people even knew what home staging was. Through education, hard work, and demonstrating results – we have built a solid reputation on bringing value and ease to the homeowner during what is usually a stressful time. Domaine offers a full array of services, from consultations to a full concierge service, for our out-of-town guests. We are passionate about bringing value to your property, value to your experience with us, and value to the most cherished commodity of all, your time.     Our Team


    Liz Robertson

    Founder | Cheif Creative Officer

    Marcyne was the 1st Master® Stager in the Carolinas, which is the highest designation in the home staging industry. She was trained personally by Barb Schwarz, founder of Stagedhomes.com, who is recognized worldwide as the pioneering creator of Home Staging®. Marcyne is most known for her easy to work with demeanor, hard work, and aggressive marketing, which is why 90% of her properties sell in 15 days or less. She was a Board Member as the Director of IAHSP(TM), International Association of Home Staging Professionals, and regularly contributes to the industry and keeps up with professional seminars to stay ahead of the trends. She is a featured speaker and often interviewed by local news outlets. Marcyne moved from the DC Metro Agrea and now lives on Lake Norman with her husband Brian, and has 3 children ages 18-26, and is the grandmom to her fur babies, DanDan and Kobe.
